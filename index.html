About Coderina

Welcome to CODERINA Education and Technology Foundation, a nonprofit organization committed to leveraging technology as a powerful tool for youth development and inclusion. Our mission is to inspire the next generation of innovators through experiential, hands-on STEAM educational programs, while actively promoting IT development, innovation, and entrepreneurship across Africa. We collaborate with private and public companies, government agencies, NGOs, and the education sector to achieve these goals.

We demystify emerging technologies for learners through practical programs in robotics, coding, and 3D printing, equipping young people with the skills they need to thrive in the digital age

Through our partnerships with schools and communities, we bring cutting-edge technology and resources to underserved areas, helping to bridge the digital divide.

Vision

To create an inclusive future where no child is left behind, empowering every young person with the skills and opportunities they need to succeed in the digital age.
